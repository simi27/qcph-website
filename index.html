About Us

A Legacy of Healthcare Excellence

For over 25 years, Quality Care Private Hospital has been at the heart of our community — delivering exceptional medical care in a warm, professional environment.

Founded by a team of leading specialists, our hospital has grown from a small clinic into a full-service private hospital with over 200 beds, 12 operating theatres, and a team of more than 800 healthcare professionals.
