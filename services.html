Our Medical Services

Comprehensive healthcare across every major discipline — with the expertise and technology you deserve.

🫀

Cardiology

Full cardiac diagnostics including ECG, echocardiography, stress testing, and cardiac catheterisation.

🦴

Orthopaedics

Joint replacement, arthroscopy, fracture repair, spinal surgery, and sports medicine.

🤰

Maternity & Women's Health

Antenatal and postnatal care, modern delivery suites, NICU, and gynaecological surgery.

👶

Paediatrics

Child health services from birth through adolescence — immunisations, assessments, and specialty care.

🔬

Diagnostic Imaging

MRI, CT scan, digital X-ray, ultrasound, mammography — all with same-day reporting.

🧪

Pathology & Laboratory

On-site lab services including haematology, clinical chemistry, microbiology, and blood banking.

⚕️

General Surgery

Laparoscopic, gastrointestinal, vascular, and robotic-assisted surgical procedures.

🚑

Emergency & Trauma

24/7 emergency department with trauma bays, resuscitation, and on-call specialists.

🫁

Pulmonology

Pulmonary function testing, bronchoscopy, sleep studies, and respiratory therapy.

🧠

Neurology

Neurological assessments, EEG, stroke management, and neurosurgical procedures.

🏃

Physiotherapy & Rehab

Post-surgical rehab, sports physiotherapy, occupational therapy, and custom exercise programmes.

🧠

Mental Health

Inpatient and outpatient psychiatric care, counselling, and substance abuse programmes.
