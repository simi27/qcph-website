Our Medical Services

Comprehensive healthcare across every major discipline — with the expertise and technology you deserve.

🫀

Cardiology & Cardiac Surgery

Full cardiac diagnostics including ECG, echocardiography, stress testing, holter monitoring, cardiac catheterisation, angioplasty, and bypass surgery.

🦴

Orthopaedics & Trauma

Joint replacement (hip, knee, shoulder), arthroscopy, fracture repair, spinal surgery, sports medicine, and post-operative rehabilitation.

🤰

Maternity & Women's Health

Antenatal and postnatal care, modern delivery suites, neonatal ICU (NICU), gynaecological surgery, and well-woman check-ups.

👶

Paediatrics

Child health services from birth through adolescence — including developmental assessments, immunisations, and paediatric specialty care.

🔬

Diagnostic Imaging & Radiology

MRI (1.5T and 3T), CT scan, digital X-ray, ultrasound, mammography, bone densitometry, and fluoroscopy — all with same-day reporting.

🧪

Pathology & Laboratory

Full on-site laboratory services including haematology, clinical chemistry, microbiology, histology, and blood banking.

⚕️

General & Specialist Surgery

Laparoscopic (keyhole), gastrointestinal, colorectal, vascular, breast, endocrine, and robotic-assisted surgery across 12 state-of-the-art theatres.

🚑

Emergency & Trauma Centre

24/7 emergency department with dedicated trauma bays, resuscitation rooms, on-call specialists, and direct ambulance access.

🫁

Respiratory & Pulmonology

Pulmonary function testing, bronchoscopy, sleep studies, asthma and COPD management, and respiratory therapy.

🧠

Neurology & Neurosurgery

Neurological assessments, EEG, nerve conduction studies, stroke management, and neurosurgical procedures including spinal and cranial surgery.

🏃

Physiotherapy & Rehabilitation

Post-surgical rehab, sports physiotherapy, occupational therapy, speech therapy, and custom exercise programmes.

🧠

Mental Health & Psychiatry

Inpatient and outpatient psychiatric care, counselling, substance abuse programmes, and child/adolescent mental health services.

Specialised Centres

Centres of Excellence

Dedicated units within our hospital offering world-class specialised care.

🫀

Cardiac Centre

Comprehensive heart care from diagnosis through surgery and rehab — including a 12-bed cardiac ICU and 24-hour interventional cardiology.

🤱

Mother & Child Centre

Modern maternity suites, level 3 NICU, breastfeeding support, and paediatric specialty care all in one dedicated wing.

🦴

Orthopaedic & Sports Centre

Joint replacement, arthroscopy, sports injury management, and rehabilitation — with dedicated physiotherapy and hydrotherapy.

🔬

Oncology Unit

Medical and radiation oncology, chemotherapy suite, palliative care, and a dedicated support team for cancer patients and their families.

🩺

Day Clinic & Minor Procedures

Same-day procedures, endoscopy, cataract surgery, and minor surgical procedures with rapid turnaround and comfortable recovery areas.

🏥

Intensive Care Units

24-bed ICU (medical, surgical, cardiac) and 16-bed high-care unit — fully equipped with advanced monitoring and ventilation.
