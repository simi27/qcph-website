Our Story

25 Years of Putting Patients First

Quality Care Private Hospital opened its doors in 2001 with a simple mission: provide world-class healthcare with a human touch. What started as a 40-bed facility has grown into a 200-bed, full-service private hospital serving the Durban metro and beyond.

Today, we are proud to be one of KwaZulu-Natal's leading private hospitals — with 12 operating theatres, a dedicated cardiac centre, comprehensive maternity and paediatric units, and a 24-hour emergency department staffed by experienced trauma specialists.

Our team of over 120 specialists and 800 healthcare professionals treat more than 15,000 inpatients and 50,000 outpatients every year.
